Islam is my Religion,

Islam… is the best thing that has happened to me. Some may view it as just another religion, but for a practicing Muslim it is as indispensable as oxygen.

I believe Islam is a wholesome religion. Along with convincing each individual, this system also talks elaborately on interpersonal relationships: how to treat your parents, your spouse, your relatives, your neighbours, etc. Islam encourages one to be equally thoughtful about other sections of society, namely, orphans, widows, needy, animals, etc.
